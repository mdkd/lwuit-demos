== Compatibility ==

* Compatible with Nokia Asha software platform 1.0 and Series 40 touch devices with CLDC 1.1, MIDP 2.0 and PIM API (JSR-75).
* Tested on Nokia Asha 501, Nokia Asha 311 and Nokia Asha 306.
* Developed with Nokia Asha SDK 1.0.
